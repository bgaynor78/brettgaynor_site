uStores "Phoenix" Concept

Concept for Higher Ed Online Shopping

This is a concept for a potential online shopping experience for Higher Education institutions. The primary use of the concept was to demonstrate to internal team members modern UX and application architecture. Key highlights are the loading of page modules with AJAX and simple interface with clarity of use. The idea was to do a complete rebuild of the structure top to bottom, hence the "Phoenix" subtitle.

Technologies used

  • HTML/CSS
  • Javascript
  • Bootstrap Framework
  • AJAX
