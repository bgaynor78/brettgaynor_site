About

A Creative Problem-Solver

Welcome to my site. My name is Brett Gaynor. I'm a front-end web developer with a photography background. "Whaaa?", you might be thinking. What does one have to do with the other. Good question. I 100% believe that creativity can exist in many forms and process is eternally intriguing.

Pursuing my first degree in photography sparked my interest in a process-driven creative workflow. Fast-forward 6 years and I undertook the process of retooling my knowledgebase to be directed toward that of the web medium. I am intrigued and driven to find solutions to unique and complex problems.

My experience consists of both web application development experience as well as website development. I have expert knowledge of HTML, CSS, and Javascript, but I'm always gaining more experience. I also know my way around modern graphics programs, such as Illustrator and Photoshop (ok, it's not REALLY a graphics program). I love concepting and then bringing those concepts to life.

I'm always interested in discussing interesting project opportunities, so please get in touch.
